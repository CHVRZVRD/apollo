About Us

Apollo Adult Day Program is a community-based program firmly based on training principles and practices that are fully-endorsed and supported by the California State Department of Education, Office of Special Education.

Our training and community integrated activities are designed to empower our clients to become more independent and self-reliant, while also becoming more involved in their own community.

Our Services

AADP interrelates, integrates, includes and connects the personal interests, preferences and choices of clients with real and simulated “hands-on” pre-vocational training activities, while simultaneously offering social and recreational experiences in the community.
